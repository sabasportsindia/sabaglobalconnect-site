Our Story

Saba Global Connect was founded in 2024 in Meerut, UP, India. We specialize in the export and import of industrial waste and recyclable materials, with a strong presence across Asia, Africa, and the Middle East.

Our goal is to offer reliable, ethical, and smart trade solutions that convert scrap into valuable resources — fostering sustainability and business growth for our partners.
